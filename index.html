Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Rosted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for rosted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Prepration

  1. Prepare quiona and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    2. Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quiona, Kale, and Caesar dressing untill coated.

    3. Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as granish.

    4. Time: 1 minutes
